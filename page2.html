Gottfried Leibniz was born on 1 July [OS: 21 June] 1646, in Leipzig, Saxony, to Friedrich Leibniz (1597–1652) and Catharina Schmuck (1621–1664).[26] He was baptized two days later at St. Nicholas Church, Leipzig; his godfather was the Lutheran theologian Martin Geier [de].[27] His father died when he was six years old, and Leibniz was raised by his mother.[28]
